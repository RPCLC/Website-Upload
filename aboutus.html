About Us

Est. 2010

Stephanie Keller is the owner and curator of the Inkling shop.

She graduated from Columbia College and worked in publishing before participating in craft fairs such as Renegade Craft Fair and selling her own work to shops across the country. She previously worked in small stationery shops in Chicago before opening a gift shop of her very own. Wanting to branch out and support the artists she met along the way, she opened Inkling.

Since we get deliveries of new product every week, you never know what treasures we have in store for you. While I'm curating products for Inkling, I always look for things that make me smile or laugh out loud. I certainly need them in the shop!
